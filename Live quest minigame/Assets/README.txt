A Mini Golf game where you need to push the ball into a hole
The game contain:
"Game" scene, 
4 scripts: Player, CameraFollower, Hole, HUD.
"Square" texture a simple empty texture 

Game Rules:
Push the ball into the End Hole with the least hits
You can't push the ball whe nthe ball is moving

Game Controls:
When the ball is standing you can rotate the camera by pressing the left and right keys
to push just press the mouse long enough to hit the furthest, as long you hold the further you push the ball
Whn the ball is moving you need to wait until it stops and then you can go again.
